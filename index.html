FAQ
Which stat is which?
Values in the top of each row of the damage table and in the left of the speed/general tables belong to the first weapon. The bottom and right values are for the second weapon. A design to make this more clear is being considered.
What are the numbers in (parentheses)?
These are the comparisons for that stat. For example, if you see a damage value of "100 (+15)", it means that weapon does 100 damage, which is 15 more than the other weapon.

A positive difference is not always better; green highlighting means better, red means worse, and yellow means that the difference could be better or worse, depending on the player.

These comparisons can be hidden in the settings menu.
